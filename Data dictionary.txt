Call Center Analytics 
Table Information 
id --> Unique id for each calls (Primary key)
2. Customer_name -> Names of customers that called 
3. sentiment -> Tone of the conversation (5 uniques)
4. csat_score -> Customer Satisfaction score (1-10)
5. call_timestamp -> Call date
6. reason -> Call purpose (3 uniques) 
7. city -> customers' city (geographic data)
8. state -> customers' state
9. call_channel -> call medium (4 uniques)
10. call_duration_in_minutes -> How long was each call 	(recorded in minutes)
11. call_center -> Location of call center (geographic 	data)


SLA meanings Service Level Agreement
80/20 SLA means that 80% of all calls should be answered between 20 seconds.
